RABBITS

Rabbits

Writer, Director: David Lynch
Costume Design and Fabrication: Tony Candelaria

In 2001 I was hired by David Lynch to create rabbit costumes for his short film “Rabbits”. I designed and sculpted the rabbit characters, creating 3 rabbits in total. In 2006 the rabbits made a second appearance in David Lynch’s feature film, Inland Empire. Utilizing the Rabbits short as a bumper for the films opening and closing.

THE CREATURE

Experimenting With Materials

The Creature Walks Amongst Us

Experimenting with different materials is another way I like to come up with unique designs, as seen here with the Creature from the Black Lagoon mask. Created using only sheets of foam from the craft store and some touches of acrylic paint, creating a unique take on a classic character.
